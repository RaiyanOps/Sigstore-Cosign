
Sigstore Components
-------------------

Main components:
----------------

* Cosign

Signs container images.

Example: " cosign sign my-registry/myapp:v1 "

* Policy Controller / Admission Controller

Validates images before pod deployment.


Recommended Architecture for On-Prem Kubernetes
-----------------------------------------------


GitHub/GitLab/Jenkins --> Docker Build --> Private Registry (Harbor/Nexus/Jfrog/ECR-like) --> Cosign Sign (private key) -->

--> Push Signature --> Kubernetes Admission Policy (Kyverno) --> Only signed image deploy 



Recommended stack
------------------

* Kubernetes

* Private Registry

* Cosign

* Kyverno OR Gatekeeper

* Jenkins/GitLab CI


I recommend: Kyverno because easier than OPA Gatekeeper.
